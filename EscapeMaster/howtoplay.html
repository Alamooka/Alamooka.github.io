How to Play



To play this game, use the WASD keys to move around.

"W" being forward

"A" being left

"D" being right

"S" being down.



Your goal is to try to steal money from the banks and escape without being caught


If you see a heart on the game window, that means that you get an extra life if you land on it


If you see a grey triangle on the game window, that means that you get can destroy a police car if you land on it



Some Rules:

- Don't spam the keys!

- Vehicle Destroyers can only destroy one car that is not moving!!

- You don't have to get the Extra Life item!

- Have Fun!
